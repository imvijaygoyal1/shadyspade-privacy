Who We Are

The Shady Spade is a 6-player card game application developed for iOS. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles information when you use it. By downloading or playing The Shady Spade, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

This app is designed with privacy as a core principle — we collect as little information as possible and keep what we do use on your device.

2

Information We Collect

The Shady Spade is designed to be played without providing any personal information. Here is everything the app may collect:

  • Avatar Name: A game nickname you choose yourself. This is not your real name and is stored only on your device.
  • Avatar Selection: The emoji avatar you choose for your in-game character. Stored locally on device only.
  • Game Session Code (Online Mode & Custom Game Mode): A temporary 6-character code generated to connect players. This code expires when the game ends or within 24 hours.
  • Custom Game Mode (Hot Seat): When playing Custom Game on a single device, no additional data is collected. Players simply pass the device between turns. No data is transmitted off-device in this configuration.
  • In-Game Score History: Running game scores stored temporarily in memory only. This data is cleared automatically when you close the app.
  • App Preferences: Settings such as dark/light mode preference, stored locally using Apple's AppStorage on your device only.

✅ We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, location, contacts, photos, or any other personal data.

3

Online Multiplayer & Firebase

When you choose to play in Online Mode or Custom Game Mode with remote players, The Shady Spade uses Google Firebase solely to facilitate real-time game sessions between players. Here is what this means:

  • Firebase is used only to synchronise game state (cards played, scores, turn order) between players in the same session.
  • Only your chosen avatar name and game actions are transmitted — never personal information.
  • All session data is deleted from Firebase servers when the game session ends.
  • Firebase may collect anonymous technical data such as crash reports and performance metrics as described in Google's Privacy Policy.
  • If you play in Solo Mode or Custom Game on a single device (hot seat), Firebase is never used.
4

How We Use Information

The limited information collected is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Displaying your chosen avatar name and emoji during gameplay
  • Connecting you to an online game session when you enter or share a session code
  • Remembering your app preferences (such as dark or light mode) between sessions
  • Tracking scores within the current game session

We do not use any information for advertising, analytics, profiling, or any purpose other than making the game work.

5

Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your information with any third parties for commercial purposes. The only third-party service used is:

  • Google Firebase — for online multiplayer synchronisation only, as described in Section 3.

We do not integrate advertising networks, social media trackers, analytics platforms, or any other data-collecting third-party services.

6

Children's Privacy

The Shady Spade does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. The app does not require account creation, does not collect email addresses, and does not request any personal identifying information from any user of any age.

Because no personal data is collected, The Shady Spade is designed to be safe for all ages.

7

Data Storage & Retention

  • Avatar name and preferences are stored locally on your device using Apple's AppStorage and persist until you delete the app.
  • Game session data (scores, cards played) is held in memory only and is automatically erased when the app is closed.
  • Online session codes expire automatically within 24 hours or immediately when a game ends.
  • You can remove all locally stored data at any time by deleting the app from your device.
8

Game Modes & Data

The Shady Spade offers three game modes, each with different data implications:

  • Solo Mode: Play against 5 AI opponents entirely on your device. No data leaves your device. No internet connection required.
  • Online Mode: Play with 6 real players over the internet. Firebase syncs game state using only avatar names and game actions — no personal data.
  • Custom Game (Hot Seat): Multiple humans share one device, passing it between turns. Completely private — no data leaves your device.
  • Custom Game (Online): Mix of human and AI players connecting remotely via session code. Same Firebase usage as Online Mode applies.
9

Your Rights

Because we collect minimal data and store it locally on your device, you have full control:

  • You can change your avatar name and avatar at any time within the app
  • You can delete all app data by uninstalling The Shady Spade from your device
  • You can play in Solo Mode or Custom Game (hot seat) at any time without any data leaving your device
